Contents

This volume provides information on how you can manipulate the topology of a model in Parasolid. It explains how to work with Euler operations, how to identify and delete redundant topology, and how to split topology.

This volume contains the following chapters:

    • Chapter 33, “Euler Operations” describes the low-level functions that modify small areas of topology
    • Chapter 34, “Controlling Redundant Topology” describes how to control topology that is not needed for a complete and valid definition of the model
    • Chapter 35, “Splitting Topology” describes how to split topology.
