Contents

Parasolid models can be used across the manufacturing process as definitive 3D representations, therefore Parasolid provides a comprehensive suite of functions that enable applications to query information about a given model. This volume explains how to use these functions to enquire about the topological and geometric information of a model and calculate the various mass properties of a model, such as area, volume, length, center of gravity and moment of inertia. It also explains how to detect clashing entities and how to test the validity of a body.

This volume contains the following chapters:

    • Chapter 27, “Enquiry And Output Functions” describes the enquiry and output functions used in Parasolid
    • Chapter 28, “Mass Properties” describes how to calculate the mass properties of an entity
    • Chapter 29, “Calculating Minimum And Maximum Distance” describes how to calculate the distance between entities
    • Chapter 30, “Clash Detection Of Bodies” describes how to detect clashes between bodies
    • Chapter 31, “Checking” describes how to test the validity of a body.
