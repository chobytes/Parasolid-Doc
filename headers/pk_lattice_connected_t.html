PK_LATTICE_connected_t    

typedef int PK_LATTICE_connected_t;

Used in:

PK_LATTICE_ask_connectivity_r_t
PK_LATTICE_clip_o_t
PK_LATTICE_create_by_graph_o_t


This datatype specifies the connectivity type of a lattice.

It can take the values:

    PK_LATTICE_connected_yes_c       The graph of the lattice is connected,
                                     i.e., any lball can be reached from
                                     any other lball by stepping between
                                     lballs that are connected by an lrod.

    PK_LATTICE_connected_no_c        The graph of the lattice is not connected.
