PK_BODY_loft_clamp_type_t    

typedef int PK_BODY_loft_clamp_type_t;

Used in:

PK_BODY_loft_deriv_conds_t


This datatype indicates the type of clamp to be applied to the tangents across
a profile or guide wire.

    PK_BODY_loft_clamp_no_c         The tangents across the profile or guide
                                    are not constrained.
    PK_BODY_loft_clamp_vector_c     The tangents across the profile or guide
                                    are constrained at its vertices to be the
                                    values specified
    PK_BODY_loft_clamp_face_c       The tangents are specified around the
                                    profile or across the guide by those of
                                    faces bordering on to it.
    PK_BODY_loft_clamp_planar_c     The tangents are constrained to be parallel
                                    to a single plane
    PK_BODY_loft_clamp_vec_face_c   The tangent directions across the profile
                                    or guide are constrained at the vertices
                                    supplied in the vector clamp. Tangents are
                                    specified around the profile or across the
                                    guide elsewhere by the face clamp.
    PK_BODY_loft_clamp_vec_planar_c The tangent directions across the profile
                                    or guide are constrained at the vertices
                                    supplied in the vector clamp. Tangents are
                                    specified around the profile or across the
                                    guide elsewhere to be parallel with the
                                    single plane from the planar clamp.
