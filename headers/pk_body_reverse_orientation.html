PK_BODY_reverse_orientation    

PK_ERROR_code_t  PK_BODY_reverse_orientation
(
--- received arguments ---
PK_BODY_t        body              --- body to negate
)


This function reverses the orientation of a body


Specific Errors:
    PK_ERROR_general_body           general body (MILD)
    PK_ERROR_negative_body          resulting body will be negative (MILD)


The normals of all non-rubber faces of the body are reversed.

This function is not supported for general bodies or non-negative solid bodies.
