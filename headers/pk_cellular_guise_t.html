PK_cellular_guise_t    

typedef int PK_cellular_guise_t;

Used in:

PK_SESSION_ask_cellular_guise
PK_SESSION_set_cellular_guise


This datatype specifies how a cellular body is treated by Parasolid.

PK_cellular_guise_t can take the values:

    PK_cellular_guise_general_c    The cellular body will be treated
                                   as a regular general body.

    PK_cellular_guise_outside_c    The cellular body will be treated as
                                   if only the outermost faces exist.

    PK_cellular_guise_solid_c      The cellular body will be treated as
                                   if the only faces that exist are those
                                   between void regions and regions that are
                                   solid or contain frames.
