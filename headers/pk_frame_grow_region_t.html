PK_FRAME_grow_region_t    

typedef int PK_FRAME_grow_region_t;

Used in:

PK_TOPOL_imprint_frames_o_t


This datatype controls the growth of regions adjacent to regions with
imprinted frames.

It can take the values:

    PK_FRAME_grow_region_always_c  Regions are extended into adjacent regions
                                   of same solidity and into adjacent imprinted
                                   frames with compatible senses.  Adjacent
                                   imprinted frames with the same sense produce
                                   a single region.

    PK_FRAME_grow_region_no_c      Regions are not extended into adjacent
                                   regions or their imprinted frames.
                                   Imprinted frames in adjacent regions produce
                                   multiple regions.
