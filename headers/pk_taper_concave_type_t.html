PK_taper_concave_type_t    

typedef int PK_taper_concave_type_t;

Used in:

PK_BODY_taper_o_t


This datatype specifies how to replace the imprints to miter to at the concave
corners where the imprints of above taper faces cross the imprints of below
taper faces on the parting body.

It has values :
    PK_taper_concave_none_c   no smoothing will be performed.

    PK_taper_concave_radius_c the imprints at a concave corner are replaced by
                              the intersection curves between the parting body
                              and a cylinder with the given radius and its axis
                              parallel to the draw direction as it contacts
                              one imprint of each side at the concave corner.

    PK_taper_concave_plane_c  the function returns PK_ERROR_not_implemented.

    PK_taper_concave_mix_c    the imprints at a concave corner are replaced by
                              the intersection curves between the parting body
                              and a mix of planes and a cylinder with the
                              given radius.
