PK_LATTICE_disjoin_t    

typedef int PK_LATTICE_disjoin_t;

Used in:

PK_LATTICE_disjoin_r_t


This datatype indicates the status of a PK_LATTICE_disjoin operation.

It can take the values:

    PK_LATTICE_disjoin_success_c      The operation completed successfully.

    PK_LATTICE_disjoin_no_effect_c    The operation had no effect as the
                                      input lattice is graph-connected.

    PK_LATTICE_disjoin_is_empty_c     The operation returned no lattices as all
                                      candidate lattices had fewer lrods than
                                      'min_n_lrods'.

    PK_LATTICE_disjoin_failure_c      The operation failed.
