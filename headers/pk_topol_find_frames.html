PK_TOPOL_find_frames    

PK_ERROR_code_t                 PK_TOPOL_find_frames
(
--- received arguments ---
PK_TOPOL_t                      topol,    --- a topology
const PK_TOPOL_find_frames_o_t *options,  --- options

--- returned arguments ---
PK_TOPOL_find_frames_r_t *const results   --- results
)


This function returns the set of frames related to the supplied 'topol'.


Specific Errors:
    PK_ERROR_wrong_entity           The topology is not among the supported
                                    types. (MILD)
    PK_ERROR_guise_not_supported    Only general cellular guise is supported.
                                    (MILD)


The function will also optionally return the geometries of the frames with the
'want_geoms' option, the owners of the frames with the 'want_owners' option,
and the senses of the frames with the 'want_senses' option.

The supported topologies and their related frames are:

    PK_ASSEMBLY_t  All frames within all bodies instanced by the assembly or
                   any sub-assemblies
    PK_BODY_t      All frames within the body (see also PK_BODY_ask_frames)
    PK_BODY_t (compound) All frames of all children of the body
    PK_REGION_t    All frames directly attached to the region
    PK_FACE_t      All frames directly attached to the region(s) of the face
    PK_EDGE_t      All frames directly attached to the region(s) of the edge
    PK_VERTEX_t    All frames directly attached to the region(s) of the vertex

This function is only supported when the cellular guise is
PK_cellular_guise_general_c.
