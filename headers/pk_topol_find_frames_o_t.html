PK_TOPOL_find_frames_o_t    

struct PK_TOPOL_find_frames_o_s
    {
    int          o_t_version; --- version number of options structure
    PK_LOGICAL_t want_geoms;  --- whether the geometries of the frames should
                              --- be returned (PK_LOGICAL_false)
    PK_LOGICAL_t want_owners; --- whether the owning entities of the frames
                              --- should be returned (PK_LOGICAL_false)
    PK_LOGICAL_t want_senses; --- whether the senses of the frames should be
                              --- returned (PK_LOGICAL_false)
    };

typedef struct PK_TOPOL_find_frames_o_s PK_TOPOL_find_frames_o_t;



This structure contains optional controls for PK_TOPOL_find_frames.

Used in:

PK_TOPOL_find_frames


Description of fields:

'want_geoms'   Controls whether an array, 'geoms', of the geometries of the
               'frames' is returned.  The array will be parallel to 'frames'.

'want_owners'  Controls whether an array, 'owners', of the owners of the
               'frames' is returned.  The array will be parallel to 'frames'.

'want_senses'  Controls whether an array, 'senses', of the senses of the
               'frames' is returned.  The array will be parallel to 'frames'.
