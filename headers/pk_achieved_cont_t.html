PK_achieved_cont_t    

typedef int PK_achieved_cont_t;

Used in:

PK_CURVE_make_bcurve_2
PK_CURVE_make_bcurve_res_t
PK_SURF_make_bsurf_2
PK_SURF_make_bsurf_res_t


This datatype is used to indicate whether the preferred level of continuity
has been achieved when creating a B-surface.

It has the values:

    PK_achieved_cont_yes_c    The resultant B-surface has the level of
                              continuity requested by the user.
    PK_achieved_cont_no_c     The resultant B-surface has a level lower than
                              that requested by the user.
