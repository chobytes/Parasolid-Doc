PK_blend_range_t    

typedef int PK_blend_range_t;

Used in:

PK_blend_shape_t


This datatype selects the definition of a blend's range. It has these values:

PK_blend_range_face_offset_c    range represents the face offset distances

PK_blend_range_apex_range_c     range represents the distance to the blend's
                                apex

PK_blend_range_angle_c          range represents the angle made by the chord
                                between the contact points and the tangent
                                plane of the contact point on the relevant
                                side of the blend
