A geometrical item is a type of entity (PK_CLASS_geom is a subclass of
PK_CLASS_entity).

A geometrical item is of one of the types:

 - PK_LATTICE_t: a lattice
 - PK_SURF_t:    a surface
 - PK_CURVE_t:   a curve
 - PK_POINT_t:   a point

See Geometric entities for more information.
