PK_MVERTEX_is_laminar    

PK_ERROR_code_t     PK_MVERTEX_is_laminar
(
--- received arguments ---
PK_MVERTEX_t        mvertex,   --- the 'mvertex'

--- returned arguments ---
PK_LOGICAL_t *const is_laminar --- whether 'mvertex' is on the laminar boundary
)


This function returns PK_LOGICAL_true if the given 'mvertex' lies on the
laminar boundary of its mesh and PK_LOGICAL_false otherwise.
