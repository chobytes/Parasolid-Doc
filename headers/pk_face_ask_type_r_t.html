struct PK_FACE_ask_type_r_s
    {
    int                 r_t_version;        --- version number of returns
                                            --- structure
    PK_FACE_material_t  material_type[2];   --- how this face bounds material
                                            --- on either side
    PK_enclosure_t      material_enclosure; --- how this face is enclosed by
                                            --- material regions
    PK_enclosure_t      solid_enclosure;    --- how this face is enclosed by
                                            --- solid regions
    PK_FACE_region_t    region_type;        --- how the face relates to its
                                            --- surrounding regions
    };

typedef struct PK_FACE_ask_type_r_s PK_FACE_ask_type_r_t;



A structure packaging the results of PK_FACE_ask_type.

Used in:

PK_FACE_ask_type
PK_FACE_ask_type_r_f


Description of fields:

'material_type'         The way in which the face bounds material on each
                        side. The first entry in 'material_type' will always
                        be associated with the back of the face, and the second
                        will be with the front.

                        A face is said to be sheet if this field is
                        PK_FACE_material_none_c on both sides.

'material_enclosure'    The enclosure of this face by material regions, where
                        a region is material if the material type returned by
                        PK_REGION_ask_type is solid, partial solid or partial
                        void. This will take one of the values:

                        PK_enclosure_inside_c:
                            The face is surrounded by material regions or is
                            inside a single material region.

                        PK_enclosure_on_c:
                            The face is adjacent to both material and
                            non-material regions, or is sheet.

                        A face will be visible under the cellular guise
                        PK_cellular_guise_outside_c if and only if this is
                        PK_enclosure_on_c.

'solid_enclosure'       The enclosure of this face by solid regions, where the
                        solidity is enquired using PK_REGION_is_solid. This
                        will take one of the values:

                        PK_enclosure_inside_c:
                            The face is surrounded by solid regions or is
                            inside a single solid region.

                        PK_enclosure_on_c:
                            The face is adjacent to both solid and void
                            regions, or is sheet.

                        PK_enclosure_outside_c:
                            The face is surrounded by void regions or is inside
                            a single void region, and is not sheet.

                        A face will be visible under the cellular guise
                        PK_cellular_guise_solid_c if and only if this is
                        PK_enclosure_on_c.

'region_type'           A token indicating how the face relates to its
                        surrounding regions.

The function PK_FACE_ask_type_r_f may be used to free associated memory.
