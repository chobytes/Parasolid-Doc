PK_LROD_clip_t    

typedef int PK_LROD_clip_t;

Used in:

PK_LATTICE_clip_o_t
PK_LATTICE_pattern_clip_t


This datatype specifies the survival conditions for clipped lrods.

PK_LROD_clip_t can take the values:

    PK_LROD_clip_keep_all_c       All clipped lrods survive.
    PK_LROD_clip_keep_connected_c Clipped lrods survive if they have a
                                  surviving lball. Otherwise, they are deleted.
    PK_LROD_clip_delete_c         All clipped lrods are deleted.
