PK_general_t    

typedef int PK_general_t;

Used in:

PK_identify_general_r_t


This datatype specifies the type of a feature on a body which causes it to be
classified as a general body.

It has the values:

    PK_general_nm_edge_c            Edge is non-manifold
    PK_general_nm_vertex_c          Vertex is non-manifold
    PK_general_opposed_faces_c      Edge has two faces with opposed sense
    PK_general_solid_internal_c     Face has solid regions on both sides
    PK_general_mixed_dimension_c    Body has components of differing dimension
    PK_general_wire_in_solid_c      Wire inside solid region
