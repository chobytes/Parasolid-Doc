PK_PLINE_t    



This type represents a PolyLINE.

Used in:

PK_MESH_find_laminar_mfins_r_t
PK_mloop_details_r_t
PK_PLINE_ask
PK_PLINE_create


A polyline is a type of curve (PK_CLASS_pline is a subclass of PK_CLASS_curve).

A polyline is a curve consisting of a series of line segments.
In a facet model, polylines are attached to model edges
in place of classic curves.

The polyline is defined by data contained in a PK_PLINE_sf_t
