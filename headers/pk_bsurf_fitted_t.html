PK_BSURF_fitted_t    

typedef int PK_BSURF_fitted_t;

Used in:

PK_BSURF_fitted_fault_t


This datatype denotes the final status of the attempt to fit a
general surface with a B-surface.
It has values:

    PK_BSURF_fitted_success_c        fit succeeded
    PK_BSURF_fitted_failure_c        unspecified failure
    PK_BSURF_fitted_eval_failure_c   evaluator function failed
    PK_BSURF_fitted_bad_range_c      invalid range supplied
    PK_BSURF_fitted_bad_tolerance_c  invalid tolerance supplied
    PK_BSURF_fitted_bad_eval_type_c  invalid eval type supplied
    PK_BSURF_fitted_u_interp_parm_c  invalid u interpolation parameter supplied
    PK_BSURF_fitted_v_interp_parm_c  invalid v interpolation parameter supplied
