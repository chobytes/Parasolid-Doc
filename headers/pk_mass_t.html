PK_mass_t    

typedef int PK_mass_t;

Used in:

PK_TOPOL_eval_mass_props_o_t


Controls the level of mass property calculation

Values can be:

PK_mass_no_c      Do not find any data in this hierarchy
PK_mass_mass_c    Find the amount and mass of the entities
PK_mass_c_of_g_c  Find the centre of gravity as well
PK_mass_m_of_i_c  Find the moments of inertia as well

The default value is PK_mass_m_of_i_c
