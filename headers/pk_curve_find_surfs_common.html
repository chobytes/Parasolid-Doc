PK_CURVE_find_surfs_common    

PK_ERROR_code_t   PK_CURVE_find_surfs_common
(
--- received arguments ---
PK_CURVE_t        curve,      --- curve

--- returned arguments ---
int        *const n_surfs,    --- number of common surfaces
PK_SURF_t **const surfs       --- common surfaces
)


This function finds pairs of surfaces which have the given curve in common.


Specific Errors:
    PK_ERROR_geom_topol_mismatch   curve not attached to an edge (MILD)
    PK_ERROR_general_body          at least one edge of the curve is
                                   non-manifold under the cellular guise (MILD)


The surfaces of faces which meet at an edge associated with the given 'curve'
are returned. Each pair of surfaces, `surfs[2*i]' and `surfs[2*i+1]',
i = 0,1,2..., intersect at the given 'curve'.

If the body in question is general, all the edges of the supplied curve, that
are visible under the cellular guise, must be manifold under the cellular
guise, otherwise the operation will fail with PK_ERROR_general_body. The
surfaces returned will be those whose faces are visible under the cellular
guise. If all edges of the curve are hidden by the cellular guise, the
operation will fail with PK_ERROR_hidden_by_guise.
