PK_LATTICE_combine    

PK_ERROR_code_t               PK_LATTICE_combine
(
--- received arguments ---
int                           n_lattices, --- number of lattices
PK_LATTICE_t                  lattices[], --- array of lattices
const PK_LATTICE_combine_o_t *options,    --- options

--- returned arguments ---
PK_LATTICE_combine_r_t *const results     --- results
)


This function creates a new lattice by combining the supplied 'lattices' into a
single, possibly disjoint lattice.


Specific Errors:
    PK_ERROR_insufficient_lattices      Fewer than two lattices were supplied.
                                        (MILD)
    PK_ERROR_bad_tolerance              Snap tolerance is less than
                                        session precision. (MILD)
                                        Snap tolerance is greater than
                                        the minimum lball radii. (SERIOUS)
    PK_ERROR_combine_failed             The combine operation failed. (SERIOUS)
