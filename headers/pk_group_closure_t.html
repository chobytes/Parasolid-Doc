This datatype determines whether groups are closed

It has the values:

    PK_GROUP_closure_closed_c       a group is closed or only closed groups are
                                    to be considered
    PK_GROUP_closure_open_c         a group is open or only open groups are to
                                    be considered
    PK_GROUP_closure_any_c          all groups are to be considered
