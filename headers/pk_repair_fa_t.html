PK_repair_fa_t    

typedef int PK_repair_fa_t;

Used in:

PK_FACE_change_o_t


This datatype determines the degree by which topology of the part may be
repaired and adjusted to make the part valid.

It has the values:

PK_repair_fa_yes_c      Repairs will be performed to ensure face is valid,
                        including inconsistences with adjacent topology.

PK_repair_fa_local_c    Repairs will be performed to ensure face is
                        locally valid but there will be no attempt to fix
                        face-face inconsistencies.
