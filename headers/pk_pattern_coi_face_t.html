PK_pattern_coi_face_t    

typedef int PK_pattern_coi_face_t;

Used in:

PK_FACE_pattern_2_o_t
PK_FACE_pattern_o_t


This datatype indicates whether the boundary loops of the patterned features
are known to be coincident with the surface of the face after the patterning
operation. It has two values:

    PK_pattern_coi_face_unknown_c   boundary loops not known to be coincident
                                    with face
    PK_pattern_coi_face_yes_c       boundary loops coincident with face
